Who We Are

ReLoop Electronics: Pioneering the Circular Tech Movement

ReLoop Electronics is a Dubai-based sustainable technology organization dedicated to transforming the lifecycle of consumer electronics. Operating out of Dubai Silicon Oasis, we bridge the gap between ecological responsibility and computing affordability.

By deploying advanced, software-driven diagnostics and certified high-grade refurbishing processes, we give computers, laptops, and smartphones a pristine second life—guaranteeing peak performance under strict, trustworthy warranties.

Our Mission

To redirect massive streams of electronic waste away from regional landfills, converting hardware disposal into highly secure, energy-efficient corporate and retail tech solutions.

Our Vision

To establish the Middle East's primary zero-waste, carbon-neutral circular marketplace where sustainable computing becomes the baseline norm for organizations and individuals alike.

Enterprise Capabilities

Our Circular Business & Refurbishment Services

A comprehensive, closed-loop approach bridging individuals and institutions to reliable, highly certified refurbished computing hardware.

Device Refurbishment

Industry-grade 50-point diagnostic tests ensuring OEM performance. Overhauling batteries, thermals, and logic circuits to pristine condition with 1-year local warranty.

Instant Trade-In Engine

Our dynamic pricing engine offers fair, automated algorithms determining value for old consumer gadgets. We pickup right from customers' doorsteps in UAE.

Zero-Landfill Recycling

Ethical end-of-life recovery of gold, copper, silicon, and aluminum. Partnering with localized smelters to achieve absolutely zero e-waste landfill output.

B2B IT Asset Retirement

Bulk lifecycle retirements, hardware leases, and secure sanitization services matching US DoD data destruction standards for corporate partners in Dubai.
