# ReLoop | Circular Marketplace & Refurbishment Network

**ReLoop** is a premier certified circular marketplace and refurbishment network, based in Dubai Silicon Oasis. We specialize in the professional procurement, refurbishment, and distribution of high-quality electronics, bridging the gap between premium performance and global sustainability.

---

## 🚀 Vision
To lead the circular economy by providing accessible, reliable, and sustainable technological solutions to the global market. We are redefining tech lifecycles by giving technology a second life.

## 🛠 Features
- **Circular Marketplace:** A sustainable platform for high-quality refurbished electronics.
- **Expert Refurbishment:** Professional testing and restoration services.
- **Growth-Oriented Strategy:** Clear roadmap for scaling operations and infrastructure.
- **Modern Tech Stack:** Built with Laravel for scalability and performance.
